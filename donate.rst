Donate
======

If you appreciate this guide or any of the other work by noahc3 and friends, please consider contributing! Donations are used towards funding servers and giving us a budget to continue expanding our services in the future.

.. raw:: html

    <b><a href="https://www.patreon.com/noahc3">Patreon</a></b><br><br>

    
Patreon allows you to make a monthly recurring donation. This helps us ensure we will have a stable budget each month to cover server costs. Patrons will receive a special rank on our Discord server and occasionally access to beta builds of my stuff before officially released.

.. raw:: html

    <b><a href="https://ko-fi.com/noahc3">Ko-fi (PayPal, Credit Card)</a></b><br><br>

    
Ko-fi is used as a frontend for PayPal and Stripe. You can make a one-time donation through Ko-fi using PayPal, or a credit card (Stripe) without needing to create a Ko-fi account. Donators also get a special rank on our server an occasionally access to beta builds of my stuff before officially released.
